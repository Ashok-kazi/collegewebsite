Our Academic Offerings

BIT - Bachelor of Information Technology

The BIT program focuses on equipping students with the necessary skills in programming, database management, and network security to excel in the tech industry.

BHM - Bachelor of Hotel Management

BHM prepares students for leadership roles in the hospitality industry, covering hotel operations, food & beverage management, and customer service excellence.

BCA - Bachelor of Computer Applications

The BCA program provides a strong foundation in software development, web technologies, and system analysis, ideal for aspiring IT professionals.

Diploma Programs

Our diploma programs offer specialized training in various fields, ensuring students gain practical skills required for industry-specific careers.

Art Studies

Our Art programs foster creativity and innovation, helping students develop their artistic skills in painting, sculpture, and digital design.

Sports Programs

We encourage physical fitness and teamwork through various sports programs, providing students with opportunities to excel in athletics and team sports.
